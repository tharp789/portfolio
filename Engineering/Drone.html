About the Project: This roller coaster simulation was coded in C++ as a project for a C++ for Engineers course. Testing and Validation Below are some pictures of the subsystems of the boat. On the top right are the two motors which operated the boat as a skid steer. These were controlled by ESCs, operated by an Arduino with an IMU and a magnetometer to detect pose. On the bottom right was our sensor package, which included an Arduino wired to various water data sensors like salinity, turbidity, temperature, and pH. Lastly, on the bottom left, you can see the reel and chute system for deploying the sensor package and some of the internal electronics, like the batteries for powering all subsystems.
